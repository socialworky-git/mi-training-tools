Intentional Reflections

Every statement below mixes sustain talk with change talk. As a group, find the change talk and craft a reflection that highlights or deepens it.

Instructions:
  1. Have one member read the statement aloud.
  2. Work together to identify the change talk.
  3. Develop a reflection that highlights or deepens that change talk.
  4. Read the statement again while another member responds with your reflection.
  5. Rotate roles and move to the next statement.
Remember: a reflection is a statement, not a question. If your group gets stuck on the change talk, tap “Show change talk” on that statement.
